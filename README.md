# Sales Analytics Dashboard | Excel & Power Query

## Project Overview
This project is an interactive sales analytics dashboard built using Excel and Power Query.  
The dashboard analyzes sales performance, profitability, customer activity, and product trends to support business decision-making.

## Objectives
- Monitor overall sales performance and profitability  
- Analyze sales by category, state, city, and sales representatives  
- Evaluate customer activity and order volume  
- Identify top-performing regions and products  
- Support data-driven business decisions  

## Key Metrics
- Total Sales: 2,297,201  
- Net Sales: 2,116,697  
- Returns: 2,116,697  
- Discounts: (297,687)  
- COGS: (2,151,218)  
- Profit: 263,165  
- Total Customers: 793  
- Total Orders: 5009  

## Analysis Covered
- Sales by Category  
- Sales by Sales Representative and Region  
- Top Cities in Sales  
- Top States in Sales  
- Sales by State and Category  
- Sales by Sub-Category  
- Sales and Quantity Trends Over Time  

## Key Insights
- Technology category generates the highest sales revenue  
- Certain sales representatives outperform others in revenue generation  
- California and New York lead overall sales performance  
- Major cities contribute significantly to total sales  
- Sales trends show consistent growth over the analyzed years  

## Business Value
- Supports revenue and profit monitoring  
- Helps identify top-performing regions and products  
- Enables better sales strategy and targeting  
- Improves decision-making based on customer and order insights  

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel  
- Power Query  
- Pivot Tables  
- Data Visualization
